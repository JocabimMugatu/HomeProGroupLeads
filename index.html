The problem with how most contractors get leads

You're paying for leads
your competitors get too.

Every major lead source has a fatal flaw.

🔗
Angi & HomeAdvisor
Your lead gets sold to 4–5 other contractors simultaneously. You're not getting a customer — you're entering a price war. The homeowner picks whoever calls first and quotes lowest.
💸
Google & Facebook Ads
The moment you stop paying, the leads stop. You're renting attention, not building reputation. Ad costs for home services keep rising with zero lasting brand equity to show for it.
🎲
Word of mouth alone
Inconsistent, unpredictable, impossible to scale. Great for established businesses — but when business is slow, you can't control when the next referral comes in.
There's a channel your competitors haven't touched yet.

Every day, homeowners in your city are asking their neighbors for contractor recommendations — and the answer is almost never your name. Yet.

The process

How it works

Three steps. Fully managed. You just answer the phone.

1
We find your groups
We research and join the most active local Facebook community groups within 20 miles of your business. On the First Coast alone, that's 70+ groups covering 200,000+ homeowners. Only high-engagement communities make the cut.
2
We post your story
Our writers craft authentic, neighbor-style posts — reactive replies to homeowner requests and proactive community content like local giveaways and project stories. No ads. No phone numbers in captions. Content that earns trust, not eye-rolls.
3
You receive the lead
When homeowners respond, we capture their project details and deliver a warm, exclusive lead to your phone via text — within 15 minutes. You call a neighbor who already knows your name.
